Task: Use elevator
- Specify your "desired" floor (1-10).
- Specify your "current" floor (1-10).
- Start the "floor remaining" counter at 0 floors.
- Every time a floor is passed, until "desired" floor is reached:
	- Say the "current" floor.
- If "desired" floor is greather than "current" floor:
	- Add 1 to the "floor remaining" count.
	- Say the "floor remaining" count.
- If "desired" floor is lower than "current" floor:
	- Subtract 1 from the "floor remaining" count
	- Say the "floor remaining" count.
- Otherwise:
	- If it is "desired" floor:
		- Say "I've made it!"
